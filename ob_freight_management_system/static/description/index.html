Overview

You may handle all freight activities by land, sea, and air with the use of this module.You may use it to oversee every facet of your freight operations.

Features

Establish Freight Orders.

Freight Operation Services configuration.

Custom clearance and revision of custom clearance.

Freight Order Tracking Information.

Set up the routes for the freight operations.

Tracking of freight operations.

Reports on freight orders.

Work Methods

Open freight management after installation, navigate to shipment, and then make the fear orders.

Developing the maritime routes.We may also add the route operations.

Defining the shipping-related services. We can also include the Services. On the basis of the vendor and the services the vendor offered, we may determine the entire selling amount..

We may establish the custom clearance for the freight order by clicking the custom clearance option. In the custom clearance option, which is found under custom, we may examine the custom clearance. We can confirm our order for fear after the customs clearance has been confirmed.

By selecting the revision button, we can make the revision for the custom clearance. The Revision smart button allows us to examine the custom clearance revision.

By selecting the invoice option, we may produce an invoice for the freight order.

By clicking the tracking option, we can keep track of our order.

We may print the tracked order report and the fright order report from the action bar.

A port menu for customizing our ports may be found under the configuration menu. Here, we might mention the port's location and its range of transportation options.

Under configuration menu we can see a Route operation menu, it is used to configuring the routes.

Under configuration menu we can see a Containers menu, is used to configure containers. The weight and volume can both be specified here.The warning notice will appear if we add more items than the container can hold. Once an order for a certain container has been verified, it cannot be utilized for another order.

A Services menu is visible under the configuration menu and is used to configure the services. Here, a specified quantity can be set for a single seller.
